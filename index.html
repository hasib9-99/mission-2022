My Mission in 2022

mission

A mission statement describes what you want now and how you will achieve your long term aspiration. A mission statement does not define a long term future state but is more concerned with the present state. It answers the questions of: “What do I do?”, “How do I do it?”, “Who do I do it for?”, “What makes me different”, and “What is the benefit?”
